5-Step Night Routine to Reduce Tomorrow’s Overwhelm

If your brain starts spiraling the second you think about tomorrow, this routine is for you. It’s short, gentle, and doesn’t require “productivity mode” to be turned on.

Step 1 – The 2-minute reset

Set a timer for two minutes. Put trash in the trash, dishes in the sink, and obvious clutter in one bin. You’re not “cleaning the whole house” – you’re just making tomorrow’s self trip over fewer piles.

Step 2 – Tomorrow’s bare minimum

On a sticky note or notes app, list no more than three things that truly matter tomorrow. Think “take meds, send 1 email, walk dog” – not “fix my whole life.”
